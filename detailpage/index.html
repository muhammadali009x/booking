Description

There is an opportunity to rent a car. Designated smoking areas are provided. You can call to the front desk and order room service. Soundproofing adds more comfort to the rooms. There is a restaurant functioning where you can order delicious dishes and beverages. A tour desk can assist you in arranging tours to the most fascinating places. Payment for services is possible with bank cards: American Express, Visa, Mastercard, Diners Club, UnionPay credit card.

Staff talks in Russian, English and Arabic.

A few words about rooms

For guests are available 6 rooms in the hotel. You can choose from different types of rooms: quadruple, suite, triple, double, family, twin/double. Each guestroom features amenities such as telephone, air conditioning, free toiletries, flat-screen tv.

Accommodation has a lot of rooms with view.
